About Me

I didn’t follow a traditional path into software development. I began my journey as a self-taught Python developer, driven by curiosity and a strong desire to understand how applications work from end to end. Through consistent practice, experimentation, and building projects, I developed a solid foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies, gradually shaping myself into a Python Full Stack Developer.

To strengthen my practical skills, I completed a Python Full Stack Development internship at Inmakes Info Tech Pvt. Ltd., where I gained hands-on experience working on real-world projects. During the internship, I worked with modern frameworks, built dynamic web applications, integrated databases, and followed best practices in coding and application structure. This experience helped me understand real development workflows, teamwork, and how to transform requirements into functional solutions.

What drives me is the challenge of solving meaningful problems and continuously improving my skills. I enjoy building scalable, efficient, and user-friendly applications, whether it’s designing clean interfaces, developing robust back-end logic, or optimizing performance. I see myself not just as someone who writes code, but as a quick learner, adaptable professional, and a developer committed to creating practical and impactful software solutions.
